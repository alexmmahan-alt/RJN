Hot & Cold Therapy

Luxury Infrared Sauna + Cold Plunge, always together

This is what elite athletes use to recover faster than everyone else. You start in our luxury infrared sauna. Unlike traditional saunas, infrared heat penetrates deep into muscle tissue, not just the surface. It loosens joints, increases circulation, and triggers real cellular recovery from the inside out. Then you hit the cold plunge. Inflammation drops, your nervous system resets, and energy surges. The contrast between the two creates a pumping effect through your tissues that no stretching or foam roller can touch.

Best for athletes, post-surgical patients, anyone dealing with chronic soreness, inflammation, or fatigue. If you feel run down, this session works.

Compression Arm Sleeves

In-clinic compression therapy for the arm and elbow

Swelling, stiffness, and poor circulation in the arm don't just slow you down, they stall your entire recovery. Our in-clinic compression sleeve sessions use graduated pressure to actively push fluid out of the tissue, restore healthy blood flow, and give your arm the environment it needs to heal. Applied by our therapists, not a machine you run yourself.
